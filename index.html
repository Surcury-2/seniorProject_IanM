Manufacturing and industry is a scarcely covered topic in schools across the nation, even though they involves a multitude of engineering diciplines.

Using this website, explore the numerous facets of manufacturing, with examples and visual cues to reinforce your understanding.

Guide

To begin, visit the "Quizzes" page underneath "Resources" and complete the entrance survey.
Afterwards, visit the "About" page to gain an initial filler into the prospect of manufacturing and industry processes.

Once you're ready, visit "Content" to learn about specific topics.
Media includes: videos, links, and images.

If you would like to download any of the content, including models, visit the "Downloads" page.
Finally, revisit the "Quizzes" page to complete the exit survey.

You may also visit the "Manufacturing" page to learn more about the manufacturing process and about manufacturing companies.
