Intro

Manufacturing is the process of converting raw materials, components, or parts into finished goods through various production methods. It involves a series of steps, including design, planning, sourcing materials, production, quality control, and distribution. Manufacturing plays a crucial role in the economy by creating jobs, driving innovation, and supporting industries like automotive, electronics, and textiles.


Engineering Design Process

The engineering design process typically includes the following stages:
  1. Define the Problem —
    Ask yourself these questions:
    • What is the problem?
    • What is the purpose of the solution?
    • Who is the problem affecting/ who is the customer?
    • What are the limitations and requirements?
  2. Brainstorm —
    Think of any possible solutions; do NOT overlook alternatives.
  3. Background Research —
    Engage and learn from others, look at previous solutions, and perform research on users or customers.
